# Design Inspiration

This folder is optional. Use it to collect references before running `redesign-prompt.md` in Claude Design.

You can add:

- Screenshots of apps you like
- Screenshots of your current app
- Links to products with a useful style direction
- Notes about typography, color, density, layout, or interaction patterns
- Brand assets, if you have them

## How to Use This Folder

You do not need perfect design references. Even rough notes help.

For each reference, write down:

```text
Reference: [name or link]
What I like:
- 
What I do not like:
- 
What to borrow as a principle, not copy exactly:
- 
```

Example:

```text
Reference: Linear
What I like:
- Calm dark surfaces
- Dense but readable issue lists
- Fast, keyboard-friendly product feeling
What I do not like:
- Maybe too engineering-heavy for my audience
What to borrow as a principle, not copy exactly:
- Clarity, speed, restrained color, strong spacing discipline
```

## If You Have No Inspiration Yet

That is okay.

Ask Claude Design to propose 3 style directions:

1. Conservative — close to the current product
2. Strong-fit — best recommendation for the product and audience
3. Divergent — more opinionated and distinctive

Then choose what feels closest and give taste feedback in plain language.

Useful feedback words:

- more premium
- warmer
- calmer
- more editorial
- less corporate
- less playful
- less generic SaaS
- more developer-tool-like
- more founder-friendly
- more minimal
- more energetic

## Important

Do not clone another product's exact visual identity. Use references to communicate principles, not to copy proprietary UI.
